INTRESTS

These are some of my faborite series

Jojo's Bizzare Adventure

Jojo's Bizzzare Adventure is split up into "parts," where each part revolves around the adventures of a decendant of the Joestar bloodline. Each part is incredibly different. For instance, Part One focuses on slaying vampires, while Part Five revolves around taking down a mafia. It starts out slow, but its a pretty fun anime I would rate 9/10.

My Hero Acedamia

If you want a light-hearted action anime, this is a great one to start. MHA takes place in the distant future, where most humans have evolved into having special abilities, or "quirks". Our main chracter, Izuku Midorya, attends an elite high school in order to train himslef to be the best superhero he can be. Overall I would consider it a 9/10 anime.

Demon Slayer

Taking place in Japan right before the industrial revolution, A boy named Tanjiro Kamado's life takes a dark turn when his entire family is killed by a demon. The only survivor is his sister Nezuko, who was turned into a demon herself. After this, Tanjiro joins the elite Demon SLayer Corps, in order to find a way to cure his sister. Oevrall, even though it starts out generic the later episodes are great, and the anime as a whole contains the greatest animation I have ever seen. Considering all that, I'd give it a 8.5/10.
